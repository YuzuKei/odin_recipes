Chocolate Fondue

Description

Complete with a range of fruit and other sweet treats. Just try not to get any chocolate on the upholstery.

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate Chips
  • Heavy Cream
  • Chocolate Liquer
  • Fruit
  • Marshmallows
  • Nuts

Steps:

  1. Pour cream into small sauce pan over low heat
  2. Remove from heat when cream begins to bubble
  3. Add chocolate, stir until smooth
  4. Transfer to fondue pot or a metal bowl over hot water
  5. Dip fruit/marshmallows/nuts and enjoy!
